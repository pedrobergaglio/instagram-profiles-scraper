1. Introduction

Welcome to our WhatsApp Business Messaging Platform ("Platform"). This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

As tech providers we are leveraging Meta's WhatsApp Business API, we are committed to protecting your privacy while providing an effective way for businesses to connect with their customers.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Business Account Information: When you connect your WhatsApp Business account to our platform, we collect your WhatsApp Business Account ID and phone number ID through the Meta-provided Embedded Sign-up process.
  • Message Data: We process message content between your business and your customers to enable our AI chatbot functionality.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you interact with our platform, including log data and analytics.

3. How We Use WhatsApp Business Messaging Permission

As a tech providers, our application leverages the whatsapp_business_messaging permission to facilitate communication between businesses and their customers. This enables businesses to send and receive messages, automate responses with our AI chatbot powered by OpenAI, and manage their customer interactions effectively through our platform.

Our platform specifically:

  • Automates customer responses using artificial intelligence
  • Provides message history and analytics for business users
  • Offers human agent intervention when needed
  • Manages message routing and classification

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our platform's services
  • To process and complete transactions
  • To enable our AI-powered chatbot to respond to customer messages
  • To improve our platform's performance and user experience
  • To provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your information or customer message data to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, and customer service.
  • OpenAI: Message content may be processed by OpenAI to provide AI chatbot responses. OpenAI's use of this data is governed by their privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as resolving disputes, enforcing our agreements, or for technical reasons.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction of processing of your personal data
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for use by children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
